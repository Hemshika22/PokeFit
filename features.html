GYM BADGES

Earn badges as you complete workouts and reach fitness milestones. Just like in the Pokémon games, these badges represent your growth and achievements on your fitness journey.

  • Boulder Badge: Complete 10 strength workouts
  • Cascade Badge: Finish 15 water-based exercises
  • Thunder Badge: Achieve 20 high-intensity workouts
  • Rainbow Badge: Complete workouts from all types
